Our Mission

The Disaster Relief Management System is designed to streamline and optimize disaster response operations, ensuring that resources are efficiently allocated and victims receive timely assistance.

  • Centralized management of all disaster relief operations
  • Efficient tracking of victims, resources, and volunteers
  • Quick response to emergency situations with minimal delays

Our Features

Comprehensive disaster relief management tools to streamline emergency response operations

Victim Management

Register and track victims, their personal details, medical conditions, and assigned relief camps.

Missing Persons

Report and search for missing individuals during disaster situations to reunite families.

Relief Camps

Manage relief camp locations, capacities, facilities, and occupancy during emergencies.

Donations

Coordinate financial and material donations from individuals and organizations.

Inventory Management

Track relief supplies, monitor stock levels, and manage distribution to affected areas.

Volunteer Coordination

Register volunteers, manage assignments, and track contributions to relief operations.
